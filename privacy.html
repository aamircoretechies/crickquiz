Privacy Policy

1. User Data Collection

We collect data necessary to provide and improve the Football Quiz experience. This includes:

  • Device information (OS, device model)
  • Gameplay statistics and answer history
  • Interaction data with our applications and websites

2. Login & Account Information

When you create an account, we store your chosen username, email address, and encrypted password. We use this information to maintain your profile, track your leaderboard progress, and allow you to recover your account across multiple devices.

3. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses essential cookies to manage your session and keep you logged in. We also use anonymized analytics tools to understand how users navigate our app, which features are most popular, and where we need to improve the user experience.

4. Data Protection Policy

Your privacy is our priority. We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketers or advertisers. All user data is processed in accordance with global data protection standards (such as GDPR and CCPA) and is strictly used to enhance gameplay.

5. Third-Party Services

We may integrate third-party services for login (e.g., Google or Apple Sign-in) and payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies. We only share the absolute minimum data required to facilitate these core functionalities.

6. Security Policy

We implement industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) for data in transit and secure database hashing for data at rest. While no system is 100% impenetrable, we actively monitor our servers to prevent unauthorized access.

7. User Rights & Controls

You have full control over your data. You may request to:

  • Access a copy of your personal data
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Permanently delete your account and all associated data from our servers

8. Contact & Support

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your data and privacy, please contact our dedicated support team at info@coretechies.com. We aim to respond to all data inquiries within 48 hours.
